ILYA

The ILYA, or Inland Lake Yachting Association, is a major organizer of inland sailing events in the Midwest. The organinization offers education for sailing, as well as membership at various lakes where major sialing events are held. The link to the official ILYA webiste can be found here: ILYA official website

Minnetonka, Minnesota

The Minnetonka area is perfect for sailing. Lots of bays, reliable wind, and great summer days make this area a hotspot for sailors.
